Site Name

Plan²- This site name was chosed because I needed a plan to plan to plan my website. My original plan was "Plan Plan", but then there was a change of plans and I changed it to "Plan²".

Because '²' is an unusual character, it would most likely be replaced with the number 2 for things like the domain name, were it to ever actually get one.
